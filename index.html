Whats all this then?

Ambience lets you! Take control. If you ever need help studying, relaxing, exercising or just need some sounds on for your pets while your out we've got you covered. Maybe you want to be in a coffee shop by the ocean, simply play the Coffee and Ocean sounds and your away. Or maybe you like listening to electric guitar in the rain, usually that's pretty dangerous but not here.

Remember you can alter the mix of you ambience by using the volume controls on each sound. Have fun!
